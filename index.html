PhD vacancy on Adaptive Data Assimilation

Max Ramgraber is hiring! Are you passionate about advancing the energy transition? Are you curious about methods that will help us to learn more about the subsurface? Together with my colleagues Berent Lunde and Femke Vossepoel, I am looking for a motivated PhD student to join our project on adaptive data assimilation in subsurface systems with ensemble transport. In this collaboration between Delft University of Technology and Equinor, we will develop an exciting new framework to improve our understanding of complex subsurface environments, crucial for safe drinking water, geothermal energy, and hydrogen storage.
