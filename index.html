March 2025

Registration open for 11th Delft CCS Summer School!

At Delft University of Technology, we take pride in being at the forefront of scientific innovation. We are driven by a passion to push boundaries and tackle the pressing challenges facing our world today. Climate change is no longer a distant threat—it is a present reality, evident in the increasing frequency and intensity of extreme weather events across the globe. Last week, we successfully hosted the 11th Delft Summer School on Carbon Capture, Utilization, and Storage, bringing together participants from academia and industry across multiple continents. We extend our heartfelt thanks to all attendees—and a special appreciation to our expert instructors and lecturers, including Pavel Bedrikovetski, Tony Kovscek, Marcella Dean, Owain Tucker, Raul Valdez, Jean-Claude Hild, Joop de Kok, Onno van Kessel, and Denis Voskov for their invaluable contributions. This event was possible with kind contributions from EBN B.V. and Shell. #CCS #CCUS #Shell #EBN #ClimateChange
